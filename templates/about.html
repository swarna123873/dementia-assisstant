Welcome to Our Application

Our app is designed to support **dementia patients** by offering continuous support and assistance, including:

Reminders & Notifications

Timely reminders for medications, tasks, and appointments.

Cognitive Engagement

Brain-training activities to boost mental sharpness.

Emergency Assistance

Instant notification to caregivers in emergencies.

Facial Recognition

Secure authentication for patient identification.

Our goal is to **enhance the quality of life** for dementia patients by offering continuous support, safety, and cognitive engagement.
